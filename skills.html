Maintenance Protocols

Progression

Neural adaptation (1-4 weeks), Structural (1-6 months), Connective (6-24 months).

Timeframes

Basics: 3mo. Intermediate: 1yr. Advanced: 2-5yrs. Elite: 5-10yrs.

Mobility Pre-reqs

Planche: 90°+ Wrist. HS: 180° Shoulder. Front Lever: Thoracic extension.

Injury Prevention

Antagonist balance (Push/Pull ratio 1:1). 20% max volume increase monthly.
