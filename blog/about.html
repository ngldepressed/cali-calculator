About Cali Biotech

Welcome to the Cali-Biotech Command Center. This project was born out of a simple realization: Calisthenics is as much a science as it is a sport.

Our Mission

Most fitness apps give generic advice. We aim to provide biomechanical precision. By using variables like height, weight, and torque factors, our tools help athletes understand exactly where they stand in their journey toward elite skills like the Planche, Front Lever, and Muscle-up.

Who is behind this?

I am a calisthenics enthusiast and developer dedicated to bringing data-driven training to the street workout community. I believe that by measuring our "Weighted Matrix" and "CNS Readiness," we can train smarter and avoid the injuries that often plague bodyweight athletes.

Why "Biotech"?

We treat the human body as a biological machine. Every pull-up and dip is a lever-based movement influenced by physics. Cali-Biotech is here to help you optimize those levers.
