About Cali Biotech

Welcome to the Cali-Biotech Command Center. This project was born out of a simple realization: Calisthenics is as much a science as it is a sport.

Our Mission

Most fitness applications offer generic advice that fails to account for individual body types. We aim to provide biomechanical precision. By utilizing allometric scaling and torque constants, our tools help athletes understand exactly where they stand in their journey toward elite skills like the Planche, Front Lever, and Iron Cross.

Who is behind this?

I am a calisthenics enthusiast and developer dedicated to bringing data-driven training to the street workout community. I believe that by measuring our "Weighted Matrix" and "CNS Readiness," we can train smarter and avoid the injuries that often plague bodyweight athletes.

Why "Biotech"?

We treat the human body as a biological machine. Every pull-up, dip, and lever is a movement influenced by the laws of physics. Cali-Biotech is engineered to help you optimize those levers, providing a bridge between raw strength and technical mastery.
