System Architecture & Methodology

1. Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)

Utilizes the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, recognized as the current gold standard for metabolic estimation:

BMR = (10 × W) + (6.25 × H) - (5 × A) + 5

2. Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)

Calculated by applying an activity multiplier to your BMR. This accounts for the Thermogenic Effect of Activity:

  • Sedentary: BMR × 1.2
  • Moderate: BMR × 1.55
  • Athlete: BMR × 1.9

3. Protein Scaling (Lean Mass Logic)

Unlike standard calculators, we scale protein based on Lean Body Mass (LBM) to optimize power-to-weight ratios:

Target = LBM (kg) × 2.2g

4. Phase Differentials

Lean Bulk: +10% surplus for muscle synthesis without excess fat storage.
Lean Cut: -15% deficit for fat oxidation while preserving neural strength.
